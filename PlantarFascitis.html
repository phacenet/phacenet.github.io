Plantar Fascitis Routine

Additional massaging to pained foot area can be applied. To do so, use masage gun or pressure with hands to masage sore portion of foot, gradually increasing pressure as discomfort lessens. Aim to go back and forth along the foot for 2-3 minutes. Then, flexing the ankle and pulling the toes upright with one hand source the plantar fascitis is at a full stretch, gently repeat the masage gradually increasing pressure from very light to light. Do this to each foot if experiencing pain in both feet.
